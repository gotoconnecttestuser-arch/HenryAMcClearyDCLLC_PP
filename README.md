Henry A. McCleary, DC, LLC – SMS Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 07/15/2026

Henry A. McCleary, DC, LLC (“Dr. Mac’s Medical Pain Management & Wellness CTR,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This SMS Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information related to our text messaging communications.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our office or opt in to receive SMS communications:

- Your name
- Mobile phone number
- Appointment and scheduling information
- Information you provide through text messages, forms, phone calls, or in person
- Customer service and communication preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for healthcare-related customer care communications, including:

- Appointment reminders and confirmations
- Scheduling and rescheduling updates
- Responses to patient inquiries
- Care-related notifications and follow-up communications
- Administrative and account-related notifications

Your information will not be used for promotional or marketing purposes unless you provide explicit consent. [1]

3. SMS Communications & Consent

By providing your mobile phone number and opting in verbally, electronically, or through appointment scheduling, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Henry A. McCleary, DC, LLC. [1]

Message frequency may vary depending on your interactions with our office. Message and data rates may apply based on your wireless carrier plan. [1]

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. [1]

For assistance, reply HELP or contact us using the information below. [1]

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

We may share your information only with trusted service providers that assist in operating our messaging and communication services, and only as necessary to provide those services. These providers are required to protect your information and may not use it for any other purpose.

Mobile opt-in data and consent information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or applicable regulations.

5. Protection of Information

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to help protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Access to personal information is limited to authorized personnel only. [1]

6. Your Choices

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP. [1]

To request updates or corrections to your information, or for questions regarding this policy, please contact us.
